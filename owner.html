Commonly Asked

Questions

Mileage vs. Percentage Contractors?

Mileage Contractors start at $ .50 cents per mile, Percentage Contractors start at 23% of what is billed to the customer..

Do i need my own trailer?

Not necessarily. You can rent a trailer from us starting from $250 weekly while we also accept trailers into our fleet that have been manufactured in the last 10 years at the time of contract.

What is the Home Time Policy ?

Selected areas have one week between home time, other areas are 2 or more weeks.

What kind of euqipment ?

The majority of our fleet are 2018-2021 Volco VNL-760, with a few 2016 VNL-760 available, as well..

What kind of Transmission ?

Most of our fleet is equipped with automatic transmissions. We do offer some manual transmissions, when available.
